About the archive

The FM Radio Archive is a Music Broadcast Collection on the Internet Archive, built and maintained by Kim Paris. It's a growing collection of live and recorded concerts, music broadcasts, and airchecks spanning over 60 years of music and radio history.

The goal is simple: preserve and share this legacy so anyone can discover the magic of music radio — from legendary FM stations to rare concert broadcasts — completely free.
