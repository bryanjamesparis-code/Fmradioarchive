About the show

Kim Paris is a live music aficionado and archivist based in Portland, Oregon. He's the creator and curator of the FM Radio Archive on the Internet Archive, and DJs Melting Pot on Freeform Portland every other Sunday morning. Each episode wanders freely across genres and decades — a true melting pot of sound, stitched together with a community-radio sensibility.
